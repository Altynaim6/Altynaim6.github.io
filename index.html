About myself

Altynai Munduzbaeva COMSEP-23

I come from Jalal-Abad. My hometown is called Shamaldy - Sai
the land of watermelon and a hydroelectric power station. I graduated from School Number 2, which is a Kyrgyz school. I lived there with my family, consisting of six people: my father, mother, two brothers, sister, and myself. I had a wonderful childhood with my family, studied well in school, and had many friends. In 2022, I applied to a preparatory school to learn English. It was an unforgettable year where I met a lot of interesting people and had fun with them. Some of them continue to study with me. Currently, I am studying in the Computer Science faculty, which presents challenges, but I'm determined not to give up. Please wish me good luck on my journey!

One of my hobbies is playing our traditional musical instrument, the komuz. It is truly fascinating how,
with just three strings, it can produce such a rich array of sounds. Through the komuz, we can express our feelings
and share the beauty of our culture. We can take pride in these things, which have been passed down to us by our ancestors
and continue to live on as the spirit of our nation
